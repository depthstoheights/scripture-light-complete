ℹ️ App Information

What This App Does

Scripture Light provides offline Bible reading and discipleship materials for missionaries and believers with limited internet access.

Features

  • Complete offline Bible chapters
  • 12 comprehensive discipleship lessons
  • Prayer timer and guides
  • Personal study notes
  • 6 language support
  • Search functionality
  • Progress tracking

How to Install

iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen

Android: Menu → Add to Home Screen
